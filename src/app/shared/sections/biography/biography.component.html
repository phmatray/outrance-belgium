OUTRANCE BELGIUM
C’est quoi ?

Outrance (Belgium) est un concept d’évènement festif en musique qui s’exporte aujourd’hui en Belgique. Ce projet est porté par un comité d’organisation d’une quinzaine de passionnés franco-belges; tous bénévoles au sein des associations à but non lucratif Outrance et EWA-Electronic Waves.

OUTRANCE BELGIUM
What is it?

Outrance (Belgium) is a festive event concept in music that is now being exported to Belgium. This project is supported by an organizing committee of about fifteen Franco-Belgian enthusiasts; all volunteers within the non-profit associations Outrance and EWA-Electronic Waves.
